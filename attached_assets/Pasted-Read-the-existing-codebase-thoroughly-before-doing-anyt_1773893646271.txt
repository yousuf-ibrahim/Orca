## THE STRATEGIC PROBLEM TO SOLVE IN THIS SESSION

Orca currently has a lot of modules planned and partially built — 
Securities Master, Portfolio Hub, Research Workspace, KYC/Compliance, etc. 
That's the right long-term vision. But right now, if a COO at a $150M 
fund lands on this app, there is nothing that immediately demonstrates 
Orca's expertise and creates a reason to get on a call.

The fix is building the **Infrastructure Audit** — a single, high-impact 
feature that a prospect can complete in 10 minutes and walk away with a 
credible, specific report about their operational stack. This is Orca's 
proof of value before a single conversation happens.

### Route: `/audit`

A multi-step intake form (use the existing step/wizard pattern if one 
exists, otherwise build a clean stepped UI). Five steps:

**Step 1 — Fund Profile**
- Fund name (text input)
- AUM range (select): <$50M / $50M–$150M / $150M–$500M / $500M–$1B / $1B+
- Strategy (multi-select checkboxes): Long/Short Equity, Quantitative, 
  Multi-Strategy, Fixed Income, Macro, Private Credit, Other
- Total team size (select): 1–5 / 6–15 / 16–50 / 50+
- Ops/middle office headcount (select): 0 / 1 / 2–3 / 4+

**Step 2 — Systems Inventory**
For each category, a select with common options + "Other (specify)":

- Portfolio Management System: Advent Geneva, d1g1t, Addepar, 
  Charles River, SS&C Eze, Bloomberg AIM, None, Other
- Order Management System: SS&C Eze OMS, Charles River, Flextrade, 
  Bloomberg TSOX, None, Other
- Risk System: Axioma, Bloomberg PORT, MSCI Barra, FactSet, 
  In-house, None, Other
- Market Data: Bloomberg, Refinitiv/LSEG, FactSet, Databento, 
  yCharts, Multiple, None
- Fund Admin / Shadow NAV: SS&C, Citco, NAV Consulting, Apex, 
  In-house, None, Other
- Custodian(s): RBC, CIBC, TD, Goldman Sachs, Morgan Stanley, 
  Interactive Brokers, Multiple, Other
- Prime Broker(s): Goldman Sachs, Morgan Stanley, JPMorgan, 
  Interactive Brokers, None, Multiple, Other
- Data Warehouse: Snowflake, AWS (S3/Redshift), Azure, GCP BigQuery, 
  DuckDB, None (Excel/SharePoint), Other
- Investor Reporting: Salesforce, Backstop, Allvue, 
  Excel/Manual, None, Other
- Research Management: VisibleAlpha, FactSet, Bloomberg, 
  Internal Wiki/Notion, None, Other

**Step 3 — Pain Points**
Checkboxes, select all that apply:
- Reconciliation takes too long or breaks regularly
- NAV calculation is manual or error-prone  
- No single source of truth for positions
- Market data costs are unclear or poorly managed
- Risk reporting is delayed or inconsistent
- Investor reporting requires significant manual effort
- Onboarding new systems or vendors takes months
- Key operational knowledge lives in one or two people
- Trade errors are tracked in spreadsheets
- No audit trail for data lineage or changes
- AI/automation initiatives stall due to data quality
- End-of-month close takes more than 2 business days

**Step 4 — Open Questions**
- "What is the single most painful operational problem you face today?" 
  (textarea, required)
- "What would a successful infrastructure overhaul look like in 12 months?" 
  (textarea, optional)
- "How many hours per week does your ops team spend on manual data work?" 
  (select): <5 hrs / 5–15 hrs / 15–30 hrs / 30+ hrs / Unknown

**Step 5 — Review & Generate**
- Clean summary of all answers
- "Generate Audit Report" button
